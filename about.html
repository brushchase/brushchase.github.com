Chase is a multimedia journalist and content journeyman who suffers from debilitating CTBS (chronic travel bug syndrome). He has explored the U.S. by van, motorcycle, and freight train, backpacked throughout China and southeast Asia, worked as a bike messenger and a Christmas tree salesman in New York City, reported on politics and public policy in New Jersey and Brooklyn, and recently spent a summer in Nepal, writing for the The Kathmandu Post. Chase obtained a master's degree from the Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY in 2019, and just finished serving as an assistant editor at American Heritage, as part of a fellowship with the Poynter Institute.
