Chase is an itinerant wanderer and adventure junkie who occassionally moonlights as a multimedia journalist. He has explored the U.S. by van, motorcycle, and freight train, backpacked througout China and southeast Asia, worked as a bike messenger and a Christmas tree salesman in New York City, reported on politics and public policy in New Jersey and Brooklyn, and most recently spent a summer in Nepal, writing for the The Kathmandu Post. Chase graduated from the Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY in 2019, and is currently a fellow with the Poynter-Koch Media and Journalism program, serving as an assistant editor at American Heritage.
